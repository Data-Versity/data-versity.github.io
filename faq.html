  1. What is this conference about?
    • This symposium is a showcase of data science at UHD and in Houston. DataVersity! reflects that data science has a wide application and that the talented students at UHD have a wide variety of backgrounds and experiences.

  2. Who are the Data Science Symposium?
    • The Data Science Symposium is a group comprised of the students at UHD enrolled in the MSDA program, students in computer science, students in math and stats, and interested faculty at UHD.

  3. Why are you throwing this conference?
    • This conference will help build awareness of data science as an emerging field of work in Houston, and help showcase students doing work in data science and analytics.

  4. Who should attend?
    • Anyone who is interested in data analytics applications that are happening in Houston.
    • Anyone interested in enrolling in the MSDA program.

  5. Why should I attend?
    • This symposium is a focus on the current state of data analytics and data science in Houston business, rather than simply focusing on grant-funded research. This symposium will reflect both cutting edge topics and real-world practical application.

  6. What is the MSDA?
    • The Masters of Science in Data Analysis at UHD is an applied degree that covers statistics and computer programming to prepare students from a wide variety of background to become data analytics professionals.

  7. What separates the MSDA from other programs?
    • Other programs in Houston are under business schools, where the UHD program was the first in Houston to be under the aegis of the College of Natural Sciences.
    • Additionally, UHD is a unique school that caters to non-traditional students. Here, the student body is diverse, and the applications in data science that are brought together in student projects reflect that wide variety of skills and interests.

  8. What topics will be covered?
    • Topics including Machine Learning, Bioinformatics, Terrain Mapping, will be covered by professional speakers. Students will be presenting a range of topics of research

  9. Will there be any vendor talks?
    • No. Right now, the symposium is focused on the ideas and applications that being used by students and professionals in Houston.

  10. How do I register to attend?
    • Go to the registration page and fill out the form.

  11. How do I become a speaker?
    • Email your contact information and talk information to santanam33@gator.uhd.edu

  12. How do I become a sponsor?
    • Go to the sponsorship page and fill out the form.
    • Contact the Data Science Symposium at santanam33@gator.uhd.edu

  13. Parking?
    • There are parking lots around the University of Houston that are hourly paid parking.
    • Public Transportation use is encouraged, the Red Line rail stops right in front of the UHD main building. Attendees can easily ride from the conference to events at the Houston Rodeo.

  14. Is there a code of conduct?
    • All attendees will be required to behave respectfully toward each other.

Further Questions

Please email us any further questions regarding the symposium.
