Do you have data science research that you would like to share at the Data-Versity Data Science Symposium? Data-Versity provides an opportunity for both Professionals and UHD Students to present their research. To apply to become a presenter, please fill out the form located at the bottom of the page.

Becoming a Professional Presenter

Professionals with data science-related research are welcoming to apply as speakers at the symposium. However, the symposium is limited to only 5 professional speakers, and to maintain the high standards of quality for the day's content, most speakers are chosen by invitation only. If you wish to apply as a professional speaker, please apply below.

Becoming a Student Presenter

Students from the UHD MSDA program are offered two ways to present at the Data-Versity Symposium, and are encouraged to submit research such as class projects, capstones, or projects conducted professionally, regardless of whether the research is currently complete. Students with excellent research are encouraged to apply below.

As a Speaker
The main Data-Versity speakers series will include five students. Student presentations will be 15 minutes each, including a brief Q&A.

If you wish to become a student speakers, please fill out the form below and send your abstract via email to santanam33@gator.uhd.edu. Suggested content of the presentation, as well as the general format of the abstract required, are the same as for the poster presentations, which can be found in the link below.

As a Poster Presenter
A poster presentation will be held intermittently throughout the day, and posters will remain available for view throughout the day's events. Please refer to the presentations guidelines for more details.
